Anubhav Silas

Guitarist • Music Producer

Welcome

You’ve landed in the sonic world of Anubhav Silas — a New Delhi–based guitarist and music producer blending ambient calm with metal precision. With 12+ years on the guitar and 9 years producing, Anubhav crafts instrumental stories that move from whispered textures to full-throttle riffs.

Recent releases include the instrumental EP "Twenty Four Hours" and singles "Sunshade" and "Psalm 24". He has been composing and producing music for clients across the globe. His work has been featured by Rolling Stone India, The Indian Music Diaries, and many more magazines. He has also collaborated internationally with GetGood Drums, JST, and Submission Audio.
