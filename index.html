About

Anubhav Silas is an independent artist based in New Delhi, India, with over 12 years of experience as a guitarist and 9 years as a music producer and composer. His recent work includes two singles, Psalm 24 and Sunshade, along with his debut instrumental album Twenty Four Hours, featuring five original compositions. He has collaborated with bands, Acappella groups, and Christian ensembles, and performed alongside renowned artists on national radio. He has worked internationally with brands like GetGood Drums, JST, and Submission Audio, and is currently part of Triquetra Studio.
