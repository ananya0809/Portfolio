About Anubhav

Anubhav Silas is an independent artist based in New Delhi, India, with over 12 years as a guitarist and 9 years as a producer & composer. With a sound that blends ambient textures, metal dynamics, and sonic storytelling, Anubhav’s debut instrumental EP Twenty Four Hours - released on September 9, 2022—marks a meticulously crafted journey through emotional shifts over a single day. Each of the EP’s five tracks weaves introspective melodies with rhythmic drive, spotlighting Anubhav’s atmospheric guitar tone and attention to sonic detail. His other work includes singles Psalm 24, Sunshade. A committed collaborator and endorser, he’s crafted music for artists, bands, acappella groups, and Christian ensembles such as Magnifiers, United Worship, and Kabeer Ali, and maintains international studio relationships with brands like GetGood Drums, JST, and Submission Audio while also performing on national radio. From facilitating music workshops at St. Columba’s School to judging competitions at prestigious institutions such as Amity Gurugram and St. Thomas. His auditory vision stands at the intersection of cinematic tone, technical precision, and heartfelt expression.
