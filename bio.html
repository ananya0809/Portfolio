About Anubhav

Anubhav Silas is an independent artist based in New Delhi, India, with over 12 years as a guitarist and 9 years as a producer & composer. Recent work includes singles Psalm 24, Sunshade, and the instrumental EP Twenty Four Hours. He has collaborated with bands, acappella groups, and Christian ensembles; performed on national radio; and worked internationally with GetGood Drums, JST, and Submission Audio.
